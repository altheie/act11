Bachelor in Applied Computing Technology

The BACT in a four year degree program that focuses on the practical Aspect of the science of computing. The program develops core skill in multimedia technology. web mobile development and network system administrations. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills.the curricululum is derived from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance(COPC) No. series of 2021

Program Objecttive:

The Program Prepares leaners with the practical skills in multimedia technology. web mobile development, administration, and supervisoral skill to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a start up business or puruse a teching career.

Specific object:

The learner should be able to:
  1. Demonstate understanding of the impotance of being an computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Practicipate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program
  6. Pursue lifelong learing for personal and professional growth through varied experiental and field-based opportunities.
